Mine
Produces a triangle item every 4 seconds, based on the color it is\nplaced on and the direction it is facing.
Belt
Moves items at 0.75/s, can accept from all directions but only\noutputs in one. Can be placed over void gaps.
Rotator
Rotates an item 60 degrees in 1 second. Can only transfer items\nstraight through.
Merger
Merges items together in 1 second. Can take 2-5 inputs, waits for all\nconnected inputs. Any conflicts will break it.
Distributor
Splits items round robin between any number of outputs. Accepts\ninputs at belt speed.
Crossover
Transfers items straight across at belt speed.
Hole
Randomly placed across the map. Gives points for items that match\nthe indicated colors+rotation. Can accept from all sides.
Controls
WASD to move, Left Click to place, Shift+Scroll/num, or R/F to rotate,\nScroll, Q/E, num, or Middle Click to select a machine, +/- to zoom.
